# Evidence Boundary

- simulation baseline: present
- hardware-derived model: present
- real device time series: pending

The first two lanes are useful and publishable as modeling layers, but they do
not by themselves establish an intrinsic hardware pulse or biological-style
heartbeat. They establish that the HRV metric stack remains interpretable across
two controlled evidence labels. A stronger empirical claim would require a real
device-linked or biosignal-linked time series that was not generated by the
local model.

This repo also should not be used to flatten the broader Codex 67
spiritual-attractor-overlap diagnosis into the pulse question. That wider layer
belongs to the architecture and interaction-artifact side of the stack. The
current repo is the HRV-summary lane that sits beside it.
